Letter from the Secretary-General

Dear Delegates,

It is my distinct honor and privilege to welcome you all to the Second Edition of VJ Model United Nations Conference. As Secretary General, I am humbled to extend this invitation to a platform that seeks to embody the very essence of diplomacy, dialogue, and global cooperation.

Model United Nations has always stood as more than just a simulation. It is a crucible where ideas meet conviction, where perspectives are exchanged, and where leadership is nurtured. With our second edition, we aspire to uphold the traditions of meaningful debate and constructive resolution, while also infusing fresh vigor into the spirit of youth leadership and internationalism.

The challenges that the world faces today, be it climate change, conflict, inequality, or the evolving global order demand solutions that are informed, inclusive, and empathetic. At VJ MUN, we firmly believe that each delegate carries not only the responsibility to represent nations and leaders but also the duty to question, to challenge, and to contribute towards shaping a world built on equity and cooperation.

This conference would not be possible without the dedication of a tireless Secretariat, the support of our institution, and the enthusiastic participation of all of you, delegates who will breathe life into these committees. Together, we will ensure that VJ MUN continues to grow as a space that values diplomacy over discord, ideas over indifference, and collaboration over conflict.

I encourage each of you to approach this conference not merely with the intention of winning accolades, but with the mindset of engaging in meaningful discourse, forging friendships, and broadening your understanding of the world we live in.

On behalf of the entire Secretariat, I extend my warmest welcome to you, and I look forward to witnessing the passion, intellect, and diplomacy that you will bring to the table.

With sincere regards,

Suvidya Kankanala

Secretary General
